Privacy Policy

Gym Workout Log

Your privacy is important to us. Our policy is to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you through our app, Gym Workout Log. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Gym Workout Log is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect only the fitness-related data you provide to help you track and manage your workout progress. The information we collect includes:

  • Workout Data: Exercise names, sets, repetitions, and workout dates
  • Weight Information: Body weight measurements and weight lifted during exercises
  • Personal Metrics: Any fitness goals, notes, or progress measurements you choose to record
  • User Preferences: App settings and customization options

Important: We do NOT collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Payment or financial information
  • Contact lists or social media information
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information only when you actively provide it to us through the app's interface. All data entry is voluntary and transparent:

  • You manually enter workout details, weights, and repetitions
  • You choose to log your progress and measurements
  • You customize app settings according to your preferences

We collect this information by fair and lawful means, with your full knowledge and consent. You always know what information you're providing and why we need it.

3. How We Use Your Information

The fitness data you provide is used exclusively to deliver the core functionality of the Gym Workout Log app. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Track Workout Progress: Display your exercise history and track improvements over time
  • Generate Statistics: Provide insights into your fitness journey through charts and summaries
  • Personalize Experience: Remember your preferences and customize the app interface
  • Maintain App Functionality: Ensure the app operates correctly and preserves your data

Your data is never used for advertising, marketing, profiling, or any purpose other than providing you with the workout tracking service.

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only

All your fitness data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not transmit, upload, or store your information on any external servers or cloud services. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • You maintain complete control over your information
  • Your data is not accessible to us or anyone else
  • No internet connection is required to use the app

Data Protection

We implement commercially acceptable security measures to protect your data from loss, theft, and unauthorized access. These measures include:

  • Encrypted local storage on your device
  • Secure coding practices to prevent data leaks
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • No external network communications that could expose your data

Please Note: Since data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for maintaining your device's security. We recommend using device lock screens, keeping your operating system updated, and backing up your data regularly.

5. Data Retention

We retain your workout data for as long as you keep the app installed on your device. The data persists to provide you with continuous access to your workout history and progress tracking. You have complete control over your data retention:

  • You can manually delete individual workout entries at any time
  • You can clear all data through the app settings
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all stored data from your device

We only retain information for as long as necessary to provide you with the requested service. Once you delete data or uninstall the app, the information is permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal fitness data with any third parties under any circumstances. Your information remains private and confidential at all times.

Specifically, we do NOT:

  • Share data with advertising networks
  • Sell information to data brokers
  • Provide data to analytics companies
  • Share information with social media platforms
  • Transfer data to third-party service providers
  • Disclose information for marketing purposes

No Third-Party Services

The Gym Workout Log app operates independently without integrating any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • Cloud storage providers
  • Social media integrations

Legal Compliance

The only exception to our no-sharing policy would be if we are required by law to disclose information, such as in response to a valid court order or legal process. However, since we do not collect or store personal identification information, there would be minimal data to disclose even in such circumstances.

7. Advertising

Gym Workout Log is completely ad-free. We do not display any advertisements within the app, which means:

  • No advertising networks have access to your data
  • No tracking cookies or pixels are used
  • Your workout sessions are never interrupted by ads
  • No targeted advertising based on your fitness data

Your workout experience remains uninterrupted and private, with no commercial interests influencing your use of the app.

8. External Links

While our app primarily functions offline and locally, it may occasionally contain links to external websites or resources (such as exercise demonstration videos or fitness articles). Please be aware that:

  • These external sites are not operated by us
  • We have no control over their content and practices
  • We cannot accept responsibility for their privacy policies
  • You should review their privacy policies before providing any information

We encourage you to be aware when you leave our app and to read the privacy statements of any external sites that may collect personal information.

9. Your Rights and Choices

You maintain complete control over your fitness data and have the following rights:

Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access all your workout data at any time through the app
  • Edit Information: Modify or correct any workout entries or measurements
  • Delete Data: Remove individual entries or clear all data from the app
  • Export Data: Back up your workout history (if export functionality is available)

Service Limitations

You are free to refuse to provide certain fitness information, with the understanding that this may limit the app's ability to deliver some features. For example:

  • Without workout data, progress tracking features cannot function
  • Without weight measurements, weight progression charts cannot be generated

However, you can always choose what information to provide and what features to use.

10. Children's Privacy

Gym Workout Log is designed for general audiences and can be used by individuals of all ages. Since we do not collect personal identification information and all data is stored locally, the app presents minimal privacy risks. However, we recommend that:

  • Parents or guardians supervise children's use of fitness apps
  • Minors consult with healthcare professionals before beginning workout programs
  • Adults guide younger users in understanding privacy concepts

11. Data Breaches

Because all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers, the risk of data breaches through our app is extremely minimal. We have no servers that could be hacked, no databases that could be breached, and no network communications that could be intercepted.

The security of your data primarily depends on your device's security. We recommend:

  • Using strong device passwords or biometric locks
  • Keeping your operating system updated
  • Installing apps only from official app stores
  • Being cautious about device physical security

12. International Users

Gym Workout Log can be used anywhere in the world. Since all data is stored locally on your device and not transferred internationally, international data transfer regulations do not apply. Your data never crosses borders because it never leaves your device.

  • Significant changes will be communicated through app updates
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please discontinue use of the app immediately.
